About Borrow Bazar

Borrow Bazar is a student marketplace designed to simplify campus life. Students can buy and sell bicycles, purchase academic books and handwritten notes, and rent premium clothes, fashion accessories for events and functions. Our goal is to create a convenient and affordable marketplace where students can easily find essential items within their campus community. Borrow Bazar helps students save money, reuse products, and access premium clothing without expensive purchases.
